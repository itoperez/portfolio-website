The title Beat Em Up

Using Unity 3D

Beat Em Up takes inspiration from arcade fighting games. Fight endless enemies by landing punches, kicks, and combos. Use WASD or arrow keys to move around and 'Z' to punch and 'X' to kick. Chain punches and kicks to land a combo and a chance to knock down the ememy. The possible combos are punch1->punch2->punch3, punch1->kick1->kick2, punch1->punch2->kick1->kick2, kick1->kick2. The game ends when the players life is depleted.

In this project I got into character animation and particle effects. Both the player and the enemy have health UI, there is a sound manager for SFX and background music, camera FX for knock downs, and a scene manager. Several tags are used to track both the player's and enemy's hands and feet (attack points) to handle collision and deal damage. I also learned how to make the enemy follow the player and attack when in range.
