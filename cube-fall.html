A mobile game Cube Fall

Using Unity 2D

Cube Fall is a game where the objective is to stay alive as long as possible while falling down never ending platforms. To control the character's left and right movement you can use WASD or arrow keys. A single jump is added that can be used anytime and will reset once you land on another platform. In the future this could be made into a power up found or collected with coins. Score and highscore need to be added as well and more UI. This was my first game and really taught me the basics. With this project I learned about character movement, colliders, event triggers, animations, audio source, scene managers, kinematic rigid bodies, and parallax effect.

The game starts with the cube character in the center of the screen. The player controls left and right movement of the cube and collisions with its environment will rotate it on its z-axis. Left and right bounds don't allow the cube to extend past the visible area, upper spike bounds trigger a death as does a lower falling bound.

Platform types include: regular, moving, spike, and breakable. The four kinematic platforms have animations with some affecting the player's movement (moving) and others triggering death (spike). They are spawned from below and with the help of a background parallax effect they sell the illusion of falling. A sound manager controls the sound effects and background music and a game manager is used to restart the game after a death is triggered.
