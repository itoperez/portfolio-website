NEI Torture Device

Undergrad Senior Project

The Thermoelectric Wrist Cuff, affectionately named the Torture Device by it's creators NEI (Natalie, Eryn, and Ito), is a thermoelectric temperature regulation device that a user can wear around their wrist. The device takes advantage of the localized temperature-sensitive area on the inner wrist to control the user's perceived body temperature, making them feel more comfortable. Our primary reason for building this device was to help combat a sickness known as Raynaud's disease or cold extremities that can cause physical pain if body temperature is not regulated closely enough.

The part that I focused on in this project was the creation of the android app for communicating with the microprocessor. The flow chart of all the pages the user can interact with can be seen on slide one, a preview of some of the pages can be seen on slide two, and a short video of a fully working prototype is on slide three.
